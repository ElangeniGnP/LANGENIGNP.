About Us

Maqwaqwada Pty Ltd, operating as Elangeni Graphics and Print, is a 100% youth-directed and black-owned printing and branding company based in Sunnydale, Eshowe. We specialize in delivering high-quality printing solutions, custom branding, and marketing materials tailored to help businesses and communities stand out.

Our Services

T-Shirt Printing

Custom T-shirts for events, promotions, or personal use. High-quality printing and embroidery available.

Business Cards & Posters

Professional business cards, posters, banners, and flyers to promote your business effectively.

Number Plates & Signage

Custom number plates, signage, and branding solutions for personal or business use.

Uniforms & Embroidery

High-quality embroidered uniforms for schools, businesses, and community organizations.

Sublimation & Screen Printing

Creative sublimation printing and screen printing services for apparel, mugs, and more.
